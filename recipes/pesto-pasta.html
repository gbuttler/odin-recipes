Stuffed Aubergine

Description

A quick and easy dish for when you're in a hurry.

Ingredients

  • dried penne
  • pesto (from a jar is fine, but you can make your own)
  • cherry tomatoes (optional)
  • parmesan to top

Steps

  1. Boil the pasta until is it done to your taste
  2. While the pasta is boiling halve the cherry tomatoes
  3. Stir in the pesto
  4. Top with cherry tomatoes and grates parmesan
